Jason

In Jason Santa Maria‘s personal site, the typography selection and styling plays a large part in the minimalist design. Article titles are large, drawing your attention to them right away. sIFR is used in several places (such as the footer headings) to allow the use of non web-safe fonts without using images for text.

ARVO

Arvo is a very good slab serif font family, created by Anton Koovit. It has 4 different variations, from normal weight and normal italic to bold italic. family and this to satisfying all of our needs for strong characters and high readability. Combined with a sans-serif body font, Arvo makes a great font for titles and subtitles.

YELLOWTAIL

Yellowtail is a really fun script font. It is inspired by flat brush typefaces with medium weight and has its type style built on Gillies Gothic and Kaufmann. It has a very unique look and tends to be legible even at smaller sizes. We suggest keeping it above 14 pixels for higher readability.
